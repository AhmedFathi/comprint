The train and validation set are adapted from the RAISE dataset: http://loki.disi.unitn.it/RAISE/

The training and validation data can be downloaded here: https://cloud.ilabt.imec.be/index.php/s/wmA8MyjpjysyiKn

Train set direct downloadlinks:
    Uncompressed png files (21.6 MB): https://cloud.ilabt.imec.be/index.php/s/itQYWDRJkqPTDNa/download/Train_uncompressed.tar.gz
    Decoded JPG-compressed files: https://cloud.ilabt.imec.be/index.php/s/XszYJntxPMinoPJ/download/Train_jpg.tar.gz
    
Validation set direct downloadlinks:
    Uncompressed png files (2.1 MB): https://cloud.ilabt.imec.be/index.php/s/3d2oe99mDs2ZQAA/download/Validation_uncompressed.tar.gz
    Decoded JPG-compressed files (93.6 MB): https://cloud.ilabt.imec.be/index.php/s/jw8e9yz6ssHRfcR/download/Validation_jpg.tar.gz
    
These files can be downloaded as such:
    wget <DIRECT-DOWNLOAD-LINK>
    
And then extracted as such:
    tar -xzf <FILE.tar.gz>
